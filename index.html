Vision & Mission

The Challenge

Current physical AI models (e.g., Embodied VLA models) struggle to obtain dense and accurate reward signals during real-world interactions, limiting their ability to improve robot manipulation performance continuously.

Our Solution

PhysClaw* leverages the power of OpenClaw agents to efficiently convert simple human chat into dense, accurate reinforcement learning signals. This enables robots to learn continuously from natural language feedback, closing the Sim2Real gap.

PhysClaw* Framework

A user-friendly architecture for efficient management of physical AI models and entities.

💬

Channel Layer

Accepts human chat inputs from various platforms including Feishu, WeChat, and SMS.

🔀

Gateway

Acts as the central hub for routing and scheduling requests across the system.

🧠

Memory Layer

Stores critical historical interaction data: failure scenarios, robot characteristics, and model tuning priorities.

🤖

Node Layer

Managed by NodeServer, connecting various entities:

  • Robot Entity: Control & Sensor reading.
  • ValueModel: Task success/failure evaluation.
  • VLA/World Model: Models targeted for RL tuning.
  • Training Tools: Data management & training frameworks.
📝

Models

Utilizes VLM/LLM models for NLU, converting natural language into actionable reward signals.
